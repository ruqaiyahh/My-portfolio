About
Ruqaiyah.

I work across branding, publication and layout design, illustration, digital art, and visual storytelling. I enjoy transforming research and ideas into visuals that communicate with intention.

My work focuses on strong typography, cohesive grid systems, and narrative-driven design. With hands-on experience from multiple internships, I bring curiosity, attention to detail, and a problem-solving mindset to every project. My goal is to create work that is not only aesthetically engaging but also functional, meaningful, and memorable.
